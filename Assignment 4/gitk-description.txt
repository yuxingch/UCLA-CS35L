
Description of the subwindows:
1. The top left corner shows all the commits that are made for the repository.
   Each commit is represented by a dot. Different branches are represented by
   lines in different colors. We can clear observe the merge point.
2. The middle window on the top shows the author of each commit. In the format
   of <Author Name> <email address>.
3. The top right window displays the date and time when each commit was made.
4. Shal ID: It shows the commit ID.
5. Bottom left: it shows some detail information about this commit, including
   the changes, the parent, the child and the branches. It also includes the
   name of the author and the date.
6. Bottom right: it shows the comments on this commit.
